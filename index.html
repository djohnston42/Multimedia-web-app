ME Association Welcome

Introduction

The ME Association (The MEA), founded in 1976, funds and supports research and provides information and support, education and training. In this role, we benefit people with ME (myalgic encephalopathy)/CFS (chronic fatigue syndrome), professionals and all others interested in the illness. Sign up to our electronic newsletter by cliking on the "Sign up" tab or find our contact details on the "Contact us" page.
